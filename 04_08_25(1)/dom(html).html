Overview

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. He is one of the most popular and commercially successful superheroes.

Character Biography

Spider-Man's real name is Peter Parker, a teenager who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He uses his powers to fight crime, guided by the motto: "With great power comes great responsibility."

In Popular Media

Spider-Man has appeared in numerous movies, TV shows, and video games. Actors like Tobey Maguire, Andrew Garfield, and Tom Holland have played him in films.
